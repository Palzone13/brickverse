Everything you need to play and create

3D Game World
Explore fully 3D worlds built by the community. Walk, jump, and interact with everything around you.
Studio Builder
Build your own games with the drag-and-drop Studio. Place blocks, paint colors, write scripts, and publish.
Real-time Multiplayer
See other players in the world in real time. Chat, build together, and explore as a group.
JavaScript Scripting
Give your game logic using JavaScript. Spawn objects, react to events, and build anything you can imagine.
In-game Chat
Talk to other players while you're in a game. Press T to open the chat and send messages.
Bricks Economy
Earn and spend Bricks across the platform. Your account carries over between the website and the client.

System Requirements

OS: Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
RAM: 2GB minimum, 4GB recommended
GPU: Any GPU with WebGL support (2010 or newer)
Storage: 300MB free disk space
Internet: Required for multiplayer and account login
